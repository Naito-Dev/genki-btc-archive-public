FAQ

What exactly do I receive as a premium subscriber?

One daily email. The subject line tells you today's allocation guidance and the current signal state. Current implementation candidate under review: strict70_to0_rule_v1. The 30% path is not adopted. The body adds brief context and a link to the public proof log.

Why is the allocation guidance kept simple?

Because precision theater creates false confidence. The current candidate behavior is kept simple and auditable, with strict70_to0_rule_v1 under review and no intermediate 30% path in active messaging.

Is this for active traders?

No. This is a capital protection tool for BTC spot holders. It is designed for people who want to reduce catastrophic drawdown risk, not maximize short-term returns. If you want scalping signals, this is not your service.

What happens when market data is unavailable or unstable?

The system defaults to a defensive posture and says so explicitly. You will never receive a signal that forces a risk-on decision based on unreliable data. Uncertainty is communicated, not hidden.

How is this different from influencer signals or Telegram calls?

Every decision is logged with a cryptographic hash before market outcomes are known. There is no retroactive editing, no cherry-picking, no "we called it." The public proof log is available for anyone to audit, at any time.

Is this financial advice?

No. This is an informational signal service. All execution decisions remain your responsibility. You are delegating the judgment process, not the accountability.
