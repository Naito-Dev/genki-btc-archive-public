FAQ

Is this financial advice?

No. This is a rules-based status feed. Execution decisions and responsibility remain yours.

What do I receive?

During Phase 0, you only receive launch notifications. After launch, you receive one daily state update.

Do I need to read charts?

No. The workflow is designed around a once-daily update and a clear state.

Why publish during verification?

To prevent hindsight claims. Real-time records are the credibility layer.

How does crash handling work in this model?

If the Breaker fires, the model exits to CASH immediately even within the 14-day hold rule (crash avoidance has priority). In V-shaped rebounds, this can miss part of the recovery by design.

Can I unsubscribe anytime?

Yes. You can unsubscribe at any time.
